## Emoji Scaling Demonstration

The emojis scale **dynamically** with the text size:

- In **H1 headings** - emojis are **1.3em** (130% of heading font size) :crown:
- In **H2 headings** - emojis are **1.25em** (125% of heading font size) :medal:
- In **H3 headings** - emojis are **1.2em** (120% of heading font size) :trophy:  
- In **regular text** - emojis are **1.2em** (120% of body font size) :smile:
